## Strengths

You're the person who makes systems fair. While others argue from emotion or self-interest, you build frameworks that can hold everyone accountable—including yourself. Your gift is procedural integrity: the ability to separate what you want from what the evidence supports. In a world of hot takes and tribal loyalties, you offer something valuable: consistent standards. You make people trust the process because you actually follow it.

Your Strengths

- Evaluates evidence impartially, separating facts from feelings and preferences
- Builds frameworks that others can follow and trust
- Maintains consistency—you apply the same standards to allies and opponents
- Synthesizes complex information into clear, defensible positions
